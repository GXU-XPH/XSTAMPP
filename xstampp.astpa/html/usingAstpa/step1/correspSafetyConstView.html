Unsafe Control Actions ← Corresponding Safety Constraints → Design Requirements of Step 1
Control Actions that have been marked unsafe can be used to define new Safety Constraints. Navigate to the View labeled as "Corresponding Safety Constraints" and double click on the cell next to the Unsafe Control Action that corresponds to the new Safety Constraint you want to define.
